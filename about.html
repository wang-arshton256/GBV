Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness, provide resources, and advocate for policy changes that empower individuals who have experienced violence and promote gender equality.

Vision

Our vision is a world where every individual, regardless of gender, lives free from violence and discrimination. We envision a society that is equitable and just, where all people have access to the resources and support they need to heal and thrive.

Goal
  1. Establish Comprehensive Education and Prevention Programs
  2. Engage Men and Boys in Advocacy Efforts
  3. Strengthen Support Services and Legal Protections for Survivors

Statistics

No. of Men & Women

As of 2024, the UNHCR reports that millions of individuals have experienced gender-based violence (GBV), a pervasive violation of human rights affecting those across various contexts, particularly in conflict and displacement situations. Women and girls are disproportionately affected, facing risks of sexual violence, exploitation, and abuse. Men and boys, though often overlooked, also encounter unique forms of GBV, including sexual violence and stigmatization. The devastating impacts of GBV extend beyond immediate physical harm, leading to long-lasting psychological trauma and societal stigma.
